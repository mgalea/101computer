Mission

In addition to a common interest to help members in pursuing their interests in retro gaming and vintage computing, by aligning with the Microcomputing Society, the objectives have now extended to new horizons:

  • Establishing a permanent display of retro computers and gaming consoles accessible to the public.
  • Creating an international training institute for microcomputing.
  • Forging partnerships with foreign institutions sharing similar interests.
  • Taking an active stance in promoting responsible gaming.
