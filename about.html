About

We are from Malta, a small country that is home to some of the most historic computers and gaming consoles that shaped the information age and our modern culture.

In 2023, individuals from various technology sectors have collaborated with the aim of consolidating interests in computing history in our country.

To ensure comprehensive representation of all interests, a decision was made to establish a non-profit organization serving as an umbrella entity dedicated to Microcomputing related activities.

The Microcomputing Society is tasked with representing interests in both vintage and modern computing, video gaming, mobile technology, microprocessors, microcontrollers, and IoTs
